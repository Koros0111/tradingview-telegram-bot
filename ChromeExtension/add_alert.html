Strategies in TradingView can't throw alerts. You need to convert your strategy into the study.

Usually, you can do it by replacing strategy.* methods with alertcondition(...). For example, strategy like this:

    //@version=3
    strategy("My Strategy", overlay=true)

    longCondition = crossover(sma(close, 14), sma(close, 28))
    if (longCondition)
        strategy.entry("My Long Entry Id", strategy.long)

    shortCondition = crossunder(sma(close, 14), sma(close, 28))
    if (shortCondition)
        strategy.entry("My Short Entry Id", strategy.short)

should be converted into this

    //@version=3
    study("My Study", overlay=true)

    longCondition = crossover(sma(close, 14), sma(close, 28))
    alertcondition(longCondition, "My Long Entry alert", "My Long Entry alert")

    shortCondition = crossunder(sma(close, 14), sma(close, 28))
    alertcondition(longCondition, "My Short Entry alert", "My Short Entry alert")
    
    plot(0)
